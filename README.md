# Hazel: Open-Source Animal Rescue & Care Ecosystem

Hazel is an open-source, impact-driven platform dedicated to the healthcare and rescue of stray animals in India. By combining a community-driven digital storefront with advanced IoT technology, Hazel creates a self-sustaining ecosystem to fund and execute emergency animal rescue operations.

## How It Works
1. **E-Commerce for a Cause:** The platform accepts donated pet supplies and items, which are sold online at highly discounted prices. The proceeds are directly routed to fund medical supplies and care for stray animals.
2. **IoT & AI Diagnostics:** At-risk strays are fitted with IoT smart collars that monitor vitals and detect severe distress (such as birth complications or accidents).
3. **Rapid Response:** When an emergency is detected, the AI system immediately alerts nearby volunteer networks and partner veterinary clinics via the app, providing precise GPS coordinates for rapid dispatch and medication delivery.

## Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Next.js
* **Backend:** Python
* **Infrastructure:** AWS, Kubernetes
